About Us

Trusted engineering consultancy since 2010

Your Partner in Building Services Engineering

Meridian Engineering Consultancy is a Singapore-based firm specialising in mechanical and electrical (M&E) engineering design and consultancy. We serve architects, developers, contractors, and government agencies with professional, code-compliant, and cost-effective engineering solutions.

Our team of experienced engineers and designers brings deep expertise in ACMV, fire protection, water services, and electrical systems. We are committed to sustainable design practices and BCA Green Mark compliance across all our projects.

Why Choose Us

  • Registered Professional Engineers (PE)
  • BCA Green Mark certified designs
  • BIM-ready workflow (Revit, Navisworks)
  • Full authority submission capability
  • Experienced in complex mixed-use projects
  • Strong track record with repeat clients
